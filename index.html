Welcome to STEMhub!

A STEM resource library for women and miniorites

STEMhub Purpose

We made this app to empower women through spreading STEM opportunities and resources. We were inspired by our own struggles with finding STEM resources. We believe that having this collection of resources will encourage girls to participate in STEM and promote inclusivity.

What this site includes:

STEM resources for high school courses, clubs and extracurriculars, summer programs, scholarships, activites/projects for kids, and information about some female STEM superstars!
